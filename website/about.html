Who are we?

We are - Claus, Jacob and Kalina – team of 3 located in Denmark.
We are long time Clojure enthusiasts and that is our 2nd year in ClojureCup.

Why 2D animation?

We found of that there aren't any ClojureScript frameworks targeting the 2D HTML5 animation and we thought that it is going to be a good idea to create one. In our initial design we wanted to simplify as much of the complex game logic as possible.

The end result is really impressive – it is able to load the entire stage from a simple config structure and it is able to listen for all the input channels (keyboard, mouse and touch) and perform easily the main actions used in the most games out there.
